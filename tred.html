A Project To Eradicate Hunger from Ghana

Ghana faces a daunting challenge due to food scarcity and hunger. This website aims to help people have better access to food by providing information on the current situation and suggesting practical solutions to tackle this problem.

The Food Availability Problem

Crop diseases, poor weather conditions, lack of resources, increasing population, and economic disparity are all contributors to the current food scarcity situation. Hunger is not just a matter of scarcity but also inequity and injustice.

Starvation In Ghana

Ghana is one of the largest affected regions. Poverty is the root cause of hunger and starvation. It is estimated that 237 million people in sub-Saharan Africa experience hunger, and an additional 191 million suffer from malnutrition.

Food Wastage In Ghana

Food loss is a real problem. It is estimated that 1.3 billion tons of food, or about a third of total production, is lost or wasted every year. This is unacceptable, especially when more than 820 million people suffer from hunger.

Food Insecurity In Ghana

The COVID-19 pandemic worsened the problem of food insecurity worldwide. In Ghana, more than 5 million people may face food insecurity, including a potential 2 million children.

Solutions to Food Availability

Improving agricultural practices, food rescue programs, and technology can all contribute to solving the food availability problem.

Information Centralization

We gather information from various sources and compile them in one place, making it easier for people to find helpful resources.

Community Connections

We offer a platform for people and organizations globally to collaborate and work towards solving this problem together.

Education and Raising Awareness

We provide useful content on the challenges, trends, and solutions to food availability problems globally, helping people be more informed and take necessary actions.
